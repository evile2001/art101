Pseudocode an Everyday Task

  • Identify what you would like to paint
  • Gather supplies
  • Colored paint, paint brushes, canvas of any size, easel, paint palette, water, washing cups, smock, varnish
  • Go to location
  • Set up easel and canvas
  • Put on smock to protect your clothes from paint
  • Identify colors of paint you will use to replicate the scene you want to paint
  • Squeeze paint on to pallet in small dollops
  • Use brush to apply paint to canvas
  • Apply background colors first
  • Let paint dry between layers
  • Wash brush in water before using a new color to prevent cross contamination
  • Layer paint to mimic scenery
  • Copy what you see at location in your own individual art style
  • Paint small details over large details
  • Add lighter colored paints as highlights
  • Admire your painting as it dries
  • Add signature
  • After painting is completely dry, add varnish if you want and wait to dry
  • Wash brushes, remove smock, pack up suppllies
  • Take home painting
  • Hang it up on your wall or give it to a friend
